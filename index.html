Triple choc chip cookie (soft and chewy like subway)

Description

I was thinking of ways to get the soft cookies from subway but at home this recipe is just as soft and chewy and easy to make Ingredients 14 g vanilla sugar 36 g Castor sugar 120 g brown sugar 112 g softened butter 100 g ap-flour 40 g almond flour 20 g cacao 1/4 tsp baking powder 1/4 tsp baking soda Pinch salt White and Milk/dark chocolate chips 1 egg Optional: vanille extract (i like to add this in my naked goods)

Cooking

1

2

3

Spoon the chocolate chips through the dough Bake for around 10 minutes. The cookies will be really soft but once you let them cool a bit they will harden
